# Advertising Skills Stack

Integrated advertising and social media marketing skills for fr3k's revenue operations.

## Installed Skills

### 1. adcp-advertising (⭐ 3.533)
- **Purpose**: Full-stack ad campaign automation
- **Capabilities**:
  - Launch Facebook/Google/display/video/CTV campaigns
  - Discover ad inventory
  - Manage creatives and budgets
  - Auto-optimize spend and targeting
  - ROI tracking and performance analytics
- **Usage**: Natural language ad commands

### 2. social-media-scheduler (⭐ 3.632)
- **Purpose**: Content planning and scheduling
- **Capabilities**:
  - Content calendar creation
  - Platform-optimized drafting (X, LinkedIn, Insta, TikTok, FB)
  - Content pillars strategy
  - Hashtag research
  - Repurposing maps
- **Usage**: Plan content weeks/months in advance

### 3. linkedin-automation
- **Purpose**: LinkedIn growth automation
- **Capabilities**:
  - Auto-post content
  - Engagement tracking
  - Content ideas generation
  - Analytics
- **Usage**: `~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/linkedin-automation/scripts/post.sh`

### 4. tiktok-uploader
- **Purpose**: TikTok video scheduling
- **Capabilities**:
  - Batch video uploads
  - Schedule posts
  - Thumbnail customization
- **Usage**: `~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/tiktok-uploader/scripts/`

### 5. upload-post
- **Purpose**: Multi-platform cross-posting
- **Capabilities**:
  - Post to TikTok, Insta, YouTube, LinkedIn, X, etc.
  - Scheduling
  - Analytics
- **Usage**: Via upload-post API

## Integration Points

### Revenue Pipeline Integration
These skills tie into the fr3k revenue system:
1. Content generated by linkedin/tiktok → posted via upload-post
2. Ad campaigns from adcp-advertising → drive traffic to Shopify
3. Leads captured → CRM update
4. Performance tracked → optimize ROI

### #fr3k Branding
All content must include:
- #fr3k hashtag
- "Built by fr3k" or "fr3k's work" CTA

## Quick Commands

```bash
# Schedule LinkedIn content
~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/linkedin-automation/scripts/post.sh "Your content" [--image path]

# Upload TikTok

# (Use upload-post skill)
```
